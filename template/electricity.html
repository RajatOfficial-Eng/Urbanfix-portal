About the Department

The Electricity Department is responsible for ensuring uninterrupted power supply across urban areas, maintaining street lighting systems, transformers, and responding to power outages and electrical hazards to ensure public safety.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Street light installation and maintenance
  • Power outage and supply fault resolution
  • Transformer maintenance and repair
  • Electrical safety inspections
  • Emergency response to electrical hazards
