About the Department

The Water Supply Department is responsible for ensuring safe, continuous, and adequate water supply to residential, commercial, and public areas. The department manages pipelines, pumping stations, reservoirs, and water quality monitoring to serve urban communities effectively.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Pipeline leakage detection and repair
  • Water supply disruption management
  • Maintenance of pumping stations
  • Water quality monitoring and testing
  • Emergency response to pipeline bursts
