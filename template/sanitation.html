About the Department

The Municipal Sanitation Department is responsible for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene across urban areas. It oversees garbage collection, waste disposal, public toilet maintenance, and sanitation drives to ensure a healthy living environment for citizens.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Door-to-door garbage collection
  • Solid waste segregation and disposal
  • Public toilet cleaning and maintenance
  • Street sweeping and sanitation drives
  • Overflowing garbage and hygiene complaints
