About the Department

The Traffic Police Department is responsible for maintaining smooth traffic flow, enforcing traffic rules, managing road safety, and ensuring proper functioning of traffic signals. The department works to reduce congestion and enhance commuter safety.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Traffic signal maintenance and monitoring
  • Management of traffic congestion and bottlenecks
  • Illegal parking enforcement
  • Road safety awareness and accident prevention
  • Emergency traffic diversion management
