About the Department

The Animal Control Department is responsible for managing stray animals, ensuring animal welfare, preventing animal-related public safety issues, and responding to incidents involving injured, aggressive, or abandoned animals. The department works in coordination with veterinary services and NGOs.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Stray dog and animal complaint handling
  • Animal rescue and rehabilitation
  • Animal birth control (ABC) programs
  • Management of injured or sick animals
  • Public awareness on animal welfare and safety
