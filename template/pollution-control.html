About the Department

The Pollution Control Board is responsible for monitoring environmental quality and enforcing pollution control regulations. The department addresses complaints related to air pollution, water contamination, industrial emissions, and excessive noise to protect public health and the environment.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Monitoring air quality and emissions
  • Water pollution control and inspection
  • Noise pollution regulation and enforcement
  • Industrial pollution compliance checks
  • Environmental awareness and reporting
