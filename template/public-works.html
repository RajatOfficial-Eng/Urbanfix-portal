About the Department

The Public Works Department (PWD) is responsible for planning, construction, and maintenance of public infrastructure such as roads, bridges, flyovers, footpaths, and government buildings to ensure safe and efficient urban mobility.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Road construction and maintenance
  • Pothole detection and repair
  • Bridge and flyover inspection
  • Footpath and pavement repairs
  • Emergency infrastructure restoration
