About the Department

The Public Grievance Cell acts as a central platform for addressing citizen grievances that do not fall under a specific department. It ensures timely resolution of administrative, service-related, and inter-departmental issues by coordinating with the concerned authorities.

Services & Responsibilities

  • Handling general civic grievances
  • Inter-departmental issue coordination
  • Delay and service deficiency complaints
  • Monitoring grievance resolution timelines
  • Citizen feedback and service improvement
